As 2026 kicks off, we continue to redefine how developers and businesses engage with telecom networks. This past year highlighted the impact CAMARA is making across the ecosystem: In 2025, “open telco APIs” shifted from promising concepts to real industry plumbing. Across the ecosystem, operators and suppliers moved beyond one-off exposure of network capabilities toward more consistent, developer-friendly interfaces that can be relied on across markets, making network functions feel less like bespoke integrations and more like repeatable building blocks.
