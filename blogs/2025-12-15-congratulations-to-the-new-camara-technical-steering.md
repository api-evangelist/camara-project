The CAMARA Project, a leading initiative in advancing open, programmable networks, has announced the results of its latest Technical Steering Committee (TSC) and End-User Council (EUC) elections. These elections bring fresh perspectives to the project while continuing to strengthen collaboration across operators, vendors, and the open-source community. Technical Steering Committee: New Faces and Continued Leadership The CAMARA TSC, which oversees the project’s technical direction, has expanded its representation this year.
